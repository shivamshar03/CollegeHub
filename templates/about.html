In this age of increasing automation, programming is not limited to only computer science and related fields. Every competent engineer needs to know how to program. This makes coding club of SVVV one of the most important clubs in the institute.

In today's rapidly changing world, coding skills are essential for success in a variety of fields. A coding club of SVVV would provide students with the opportunity to learn and practice coding, network with other students who are interested in coding, and participate in coding competitions and hackathons.

Unite, Innovate, learn Together: Our Club is a vibrant ecosystem where aspiring and seasoned coderscollaborate seamlessly, fuelled by the passion for technology. Together, we embark on a journey of continuous learning, transforming ideas into reality and pushing the boundaries of innovation, while nurturing a spirit of camaraderie and excellence.
